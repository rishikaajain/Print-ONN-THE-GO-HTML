Print-OnTheGo

It is a Full stack Product based project which will act as a bridge between the vendors and customers to manage all the available printers and allocate appropriate printers to the customer according to their location. It will assist customers to print their document remotely by requesting the vendors via the application and according to their selected printer properties. Using AWS or Google cloud to efficiently utilize cloud resources by load balancing and queue management.
